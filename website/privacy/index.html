Privacy Policy

Chats stay on your device.

Yemma 4 is designed to run locally on the iPhone. We do not send your conversations to a third-party AI service, and we do not operate servers that receive your chats for inference.

Short version

The app processes prompts, attachments, and model output on-device. If you download the model, that download comes from Hugging Face so the model can run locally on your phone. The download is for model files only and does not include your chat content.

What we collect

  • Chat history: if you keep conversation history enabled, chats are stored locally on your device. You can delete them in the app.
  • App settings: preferences such as model and interface settings may be saved locally on your device.
  • Diagnostics: the app may keep local diagnostics or debug state on device to help with troubleshooting. We do not upload that data to a third-party AI service.

What we do not do

  • We do not transmit chat content, images, or attachments to a third-party AI service.
  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We do not use advertising trackers or cross-app analytics for chat data.
  • We do not require an account to use the app.

Your choices

  • You can delete your conversation history from inside the app.
  • You can delete the downloaded model from inside the app if you want to reclaim storage.
  • If you contact support, only the information you choose to send in that message is shared with us.

Contact

Questions about privacy can be sent to justin@avmillabs.com. For general support, see the support page.
